The Problem We're Solving

The average American owns four credit cards, each with different rewards and perks. Yet most people struggle to remember which card offers the best benefits for each purchase, leaving money on the table every time they pay.

Studies show that the average American owns four credit cards (Experian, 2022), yet many fail to optimize their usage. Even though each card offers different rewards/perks, most are clueless when trying to remember which one does which, even though they own these cards due to the benefits they offer.

Our Solution

ReWallet is the first digital wallet to proactively guide users towards optimal card use before a purchase is made, using AI to maximize your rewards and savings.

Our AI-powered app makes it simple to maximize your credit card rewards with just a few taps.

2. Shop Normally

AI-Powered Recommendations

Our machine learning algorithm analyzes multiple factors to recommend the best card:

  • Merchant category and location data
  • Current card promotions and special offers
  • Your spending patterns and preferences
  • Reward point values and redemption options
